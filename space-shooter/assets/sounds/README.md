# Sound Files

Place your sound files in this directory:

## Required Files:
- `background.wav` - Background music (6.8 MB)
- `enemy-hit.wav` - Sound when bullets hit enemies (35.0 kB)
- `explosion.wav` - Sound when player collides with enemy (48.4 kB)
- `juggernaut-hit.wav` - Special enemy hit sound (48.1 kB)
- `powerup.wav` - Power-up collection sound (48.2 kB)
- `shoot.wav` - Bullet firing sound (14.9 kB)

## Audio Settings:
- Background music: 30% volume, loops continuously
- Enemy hit: 60% volume
- Explosion: 70% volume
- Juggernaut hit: 80% volume
- Power-up: 50% volume
- Shoot: 40% volume

## Browser Compatibility:
The game uses a click-to-start screen to handle browser autoplay restrictions.
All sounds are configured to work with modern web browsers.
